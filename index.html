Technasium

Technasium is a Dutch programme to introduce students from the age 13-18 to both technical studies and trade and industry, by facilitating students to search for projects at real-life companies, and to actually completing these projects.

2D Physics Engine

A simple 2d impulse based physics engine programmed using Python. It handles linear and angular motion and rigid body collisions with friction between any 2D polygons, but works best for convex ones.
